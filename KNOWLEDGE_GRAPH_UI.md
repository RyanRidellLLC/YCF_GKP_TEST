# Knowledge Graph Tracker - Simplified UI

## What Changed

The UI has been completely redesigned to be simple and intuitive - so easy a 5-year-old could use it!

## New User Flow

### Step 1: Search
- Large, clear search bar
- Example searches to click on
- Simple tips on how to search
- Just type your name and click "Search"

### Step 2: Select Your Entity
- Visual cards showing search results
- Clear indication if you already have a Knowledge Panel
- Photos and descriptions make it easy to identify the right one
- Just click on your result

### Step 3: Start Tracking & Learn How to Claim
- Summary of what you selected
- **Big section explaining how to claim your Knowledge Panel with Google**
  - Step-by-step instructions in plain English
  - Direct link to Google's official claiming guide
  - One-click copy of the claim URL
- Shows exactly what will be tracked
- Toggle for email notifications
- One big "Start Tracking" button

## Key Features

### Visual Step Indicator
- Shows exactly where you are in the process (1, 2, 3)
- Progress bar between steps
- Completed steps turn green

### Clear Instructions Throughout
- Every screen tells you exactly what to do
- No confusing buttons or options
- Everything is labeled in plain language

### How to Claim Section
When someone has a Knowledge Panel, we show them:
1. A highlighted box explaining they need to claim it
2. Simple 4-step instructions
3. A button linking to Google's official guide
4. A button to copy the claim URL

### Colors & Design
- Blue for actions (search, track)
- Green for success/active panels
- Amber/yellow for important info about claiming
- Clean, modern design with plenty of white space

## What Users See

1. **Search Screen**: Big search box with "Enter your name or brand"
2. **Results Screen**: Cards showing "This is you" vs "This is your business"
3. **Tracking Screen**:
   - Confirmation of what they selected
   - BIG amber box: "Important: Claim Your Knowledge Panel"
   - Step-by-step claiming instructions
   - What we'll track for them
   - One button: "Start Tracking"

## Mobile Friendly
- Works perfectly on phones and tablets
- Big touch-friendly buttons
- Responsive layout

## No Confusion
- One action per screen
- Clear "Back" buttons if they made a mistake
- No hidden menus or settings
- Every button says exactly what it does
